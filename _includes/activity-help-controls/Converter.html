  • On the right side of the activity is a text field where you can enter a decimal. After you press enter, the decimal equivalent will be displayed below the text field:
  • You can specify if your decimal has repeating digits. Check the checkbox beside the words "The last" and enter the number of digits that repeat. Then type your decimal number in the box and press enter. For instacnce, suppose you wanted to convert .011111111..... to a fraction. You would check the checkbox and type "1" as the number of digits that repeat. Then you would type .01 in the decimal entry box and press enter.
