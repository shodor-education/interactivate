Student: Are alternate interior angles the ones that are inside the lines and on opposite sides from each other?

Mentor: Yes, alternate interior angles are angles that are inside the parallel lines and on opposite sides of the transversal. Now, who thinks they can guess what alternate exterior angles are?

Student: They are the angles on the outsides of the parallel lines and on opposite sides of the transversal.

Mentor: Correct again. Who can give me an example?
